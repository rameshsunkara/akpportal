  • Date Sep 9th 2013

Vinayaka Chavithi Celebrations

Like every year, Vinayaka Chavithi is celebrated very grandly this year too. This year "Devudi Bandi" tender was quoted for a whoomping amount of Rs.43,500 by our president Sri. Karuturi NaraSimha Rao. The food donation was organized on 11th day very grandly with the help of all the residents.

  • Date August 18th 2013

Samaykyandhra Protests

People from our village protested againist the division of our state in Ravulapalem by holding a huge rally on National Highway 5.Many school children were also participated in this protest.

  • Date August 15th 2013

Independence Day Celebrations

Independence day is celebrated in our Hight school. The event is presided by our president Sri. Karuturi Narasimha Rao.

Paritala Blood Camp

A blood camp was organized by Telegu Desam Party on the eve of Late Sri Parital Ravindra Ex.Minister. Many people actively participated and donated blood.
