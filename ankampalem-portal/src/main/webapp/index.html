Ankampalem - A beautiful village in Delta Region of River Godavari

It's name derived from .......

Ankampalem is a Village in Atreyapuram Mandal in East Godavari District of Andhra Pradesh State, India. It belongs to Andhra region . It is located 57 KM towards west from District head quarters Kakinada. 4 KM from Atreyapuram. 418 KM from State capital Hyderabad.
